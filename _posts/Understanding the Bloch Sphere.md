We can write any quantum state $\ket{\varphi}$ as
$$
\ket{\varphi} = \cos\left( \frac{\theta}{2} \right)\ket{0} + e^{i \varphi}\sin\left( \frac{\theta}{2} \right)\ket{1}  
$$
where $0 \leq \theta \leq \pi$ and $0 \leq \varphi \lt 2\pi$
